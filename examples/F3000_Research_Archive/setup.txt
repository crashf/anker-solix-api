Discovered MQTT Message Types:
=============================

0857 - state_info (Basic status)
- a1: status_code

0401 - param_info (Real-time power data)  
- a1: status_code
- a2: ac_output_enabled (CONFIRMED: 0=off, 1=on)
- a3: power_consumption (CONFIRMED: AC power in watts, 0-1229W observed)
- a4: power_value_3 (Unknown)
- a5: power_value_4 (Unknown)  
- a6: ac_ready_flag (CONFIRMED: stays 1 when AC ready)

0421 - param_info (Detailed configuration)
- a1: status_code
- a2: device_identifier (33-byte device string)
- a3: power_consumption (CONFIRMED: AC power consumption, 0-1229W)
- a6: usb_output_power (CONFIRMED: Total USB power, smart power management)
- af: usb_power_indicator (CONFIRMED: USB power confirmation field)
- aa: unknown_status_indicator (shows 01:00:00 during operation)

0889 - param_info (Additional parameters)
- a1: status_code  
- a2: param_1
- a3: param_2
- a4: battery_soc (potential)
- a5: param_3
- a6: param_4
- fe: msg_timestamp
